# Workforce and Attrition Analysis

**Domain:** HR Analytics

## Business Requirement
The HR leadership team required a centralized dashboard to monitor workforce metrics and analyze employee attrition trends.

## Key Metrics
- Headcount growth  
- Attrition rate  
- Gender distribution  
- Salary distribution  
- Work mode (Onsite / Hybrid / Remote)  
- Facility benefits usage (Cab & Food)

## Attrition Analysis
Attrition trends analyzed across:
- Year  
- Department  
- Role Level  
- Gender  
- Experience Band  

## Tools Used
Power BI, Excel

Insights: 

> Highest Exits: 
  • IT 
  • Marketing 
  • Finance 
  
Possible reasons: 
  • Competitive market demand 
  • Skill mobility 
  • Better external opportunities 
  
HR should prioritize retention strategies in these departments. 


> Gender Distribution Insights 
Female Employees: 343 
Male Employees: 322 
The workforce shows a strong gender balance, a good diversity indicator.

>  Top Reasons for Attrition 
  1. Work Pressure (~31%) 
  2. Better Opportunity (~30%) 
  3. Career Transition 

� Insight: 
Over 60% of exits are: 
  • Work pressure related 
  • Opportunity driven 

This indicates: 
  • Compensation benchmarking required 
  • Workload balancing needed 
• Internal career mobility improvements needed
