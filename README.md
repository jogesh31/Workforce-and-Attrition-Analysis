# Workforce-and-Attrition-Analysis

Domain: HR Analytics 

Business Requirement 

The HR leadership team wanted: 
o A centralized workforce overview dashboard 
o Real-time visibility into: 
o Headcount growth 
o Attrition rate 
o Gender distribution 
o Salary distribution 
o Work mode breakdown (Onsite / Hybrid / Remote) 
o Facility benefits usage (Cab & Food) 
• Attrition trend analysis across: 
o Year 
o Department 
o Role Level 
o Gender 
o Experience Band 
o Identification of top attrition drivers 
o KPI tracking for leadership decision-making

Insights: 

> Highest Exits: 
  • IT 
  • Marketing 
  • Finance 
  
Possible reasons: 
  • Competitive market demand 
  • Skill mobility 
  • Better external opportunities 
  
HR should prioritize retention strategies in these departments. 


> Gender Distribution Insights 
Female Employees: 343 
Male Employees: 322 
The workforce shows a strong gender balance, a good diversity indicator.

>  Top Reasons for Attrition 
  1. Work Pressure (~31%) 
  2. Better Opportunity (~30%) 
  3. Career Transition 

� Insight: 
Over 60% of exits are: 
  • Work pressure related 
  • Opportunity driven 

This indicates: 
  • Compensation benchmarking required 
  • Workload balancing needed 
• Internal career mobility improvements needed
